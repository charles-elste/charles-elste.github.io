Family-Oriented Business

Elste Capital is based upon strong family values. With over a decade's experience in real estate, our primary goal as a business is to build the wealth of our investors. Achieving our goal, investors will obtain the opportunity to work less, live a fulfilling life, spending more time with family and friends.

Passive Investing

Our plan at Elste Capital is to provide diversified, passive investing with favorable returns and low risk. We value transparency and efficacy. Our intent is to acquire property underperforming in rent, occupancy, and management. In return, creating incentive to lease from us while elevating rents to market value.
