Severin Elste

Severin Elste, founder and manager of Elste Capital, has spent the last 5 years gaining experience and knowledge in construction management. He has worked alongside his father and partner, Ed Elste, for over the past decade helping to remodel, flip and manage single family and small multi-family homes. After graduating from California Polytechnic State University, San Luis Obispo, he quickly realized we needed to turn our focus to the larger side of multifamily apartments.

Ed Elste

Ed Elste, father of severin elste, has spent the better part of this last decade analyzing, closing and managing investment properties . He has spent the last 25 years in sales, and most recently in medical equipment sales. With excipients in single, small multi-family and even storage, he is actively chasing his passion to grow into larger apartment complexes.
